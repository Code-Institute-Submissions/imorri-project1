Dual Sport & Adventure Bikes

Dual Sport and Aveture bikes are a specific catagour of motrocycles. In recent years that have become incresingling popoular. They are intended for the adventurous time who require a bike capable of both on road and off road riding. The general idea of a go anywhere bike for long touring adventures. Dual Sport were the original aventure bike, basically the early bikes were off road bikes with headlight and bigger fuel tanks. Adventure biked came along with more refinment and specificationsm such as ABS and, bigger engines and better road handling.
