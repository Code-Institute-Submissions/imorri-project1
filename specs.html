Kawasaki KLE500 Model & Veriants

Manufactured between 1991 - 2007 there have been 16 veriants with minor changes between most of them. There have been two main models. The first model was manufactured for 12 years between 1991 (model KLE500-A1) - 2003 (Model KLE500-A13). It was reintroduced in 2005 with updated fairing and better emissions but was discontinued in 2007 (2005 - KLE500-B1P), (2006 - KLE500-B6F), (2007 - KLE500 - B7F).
